## REMOVED Requirements

### Requirement: Loaded instruction artifacts preserve the closed-world scope firewall

**Reason**: Freezing every task, write path, artifact, and correction before the first candidate mutation conflates implementation planning with user-owned product scope. It can turn a finite qualification-attempt failure into a process-only block even when necessary local reversible repair remains inside the accepted outcome.

**Migration**: Replace the closed-world file/task firewall with the outcome-and-protected-boundary authority model. Preserve leaf-role non-authorization, candidate evidence invalidation, finite per-attempt qualification, unrelated-work protection, and explicit owner authority for true semantic or protected-boundary expansion.

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Loaded instruction artifacts preserve outcome-first authority and protected boundaries

The loaded global routing, canonical Change-Ready skill, qualification role agents, shared reviewer contract, reusable project guidance, project template, Universal Development Loop, and repository maintenance guidance SHALL preserve one non-contradictory authority model: the accepted outcome, operating envelope, non-goals, non-deferrable invariants, and protected boundaries are stable; necessary local reversible dependency closure remains autonomous; findings and reviewers remain non-authorizing; candidate mutation invalidates affected evidence; and finite qualification attempts SHALL NOT become process-only root-goal blockers.

`global/AGENTS.md` SHALL own the concise always-loaded autonomy and blocker-handoff invariants. `global/skills/change-ready-sdlc/SKILL.md` SHALL own qualification-attempt transitions, failure routing, and technical-debt dispositions. Other runtime and project-facing artifacts SHALL carry only role-specific or mirror text required to avoid contradiction. The implementation SHALL replace superseded closed-world and terminal-root wording rather than append another override layer.

Existing deterministic contract and validator owners SHALL enforce configured surface presence, canonical placement, protected-boundary markers, local reversible dependency-closure markers, per-attempt terminal semantics, and the prohibition on user approval solely for revision or correction counters. Deterministic tooling SHALL NOT infer natural-language necessity, reversibility, root cause, safety, or risk acceptance.

#### Scenario: canonical surfaces distinguish attempt from root goal

- **WHEN** strict validation inspects `global/AGENTS.md` and the canonical Change-Ready skill
- **THEN** both SHALL state that serious failure can terminate the current qualification attempt without automatically terminating the unfinished root goal
- **AND** SHALL route authorized local reversible repair without owner approval of an internal revision.

#### Scenario: leaf findings remain non-authorizing

- **WHEN** SDET, final review, delivery review, or another leaf specialist reports blocking evidence
- **THEN** the role SHALL remain unable to mutate the candidate, expand product semantics, or ask the user
- **AND** the main orchestrator SHALL own diagnosis, dependency-closure classification, residual disposition, and true-blocker escalation.

#### Scenario: project-facing mirrors do not retain closed-world contradiction

- **WHEN** reusable project guidance, the project template, Universal Development Loop, and repository maintenance guidance are compared with loaded authority
- **THEN** none SHALL require owner approval solely because a necessary local reversible repair adds a task, write path, artifact, or internal revision
- **AND** each applicable surface SHALL preserve protected-boundary approval and unrelated-work safety.

#### Scenario: validator rejects process-only approval language

- **WHEN** a configured authority surface requires user approval solely because a correction budget, final review, delivery review, attempt stop line, or internal revision ended
- **THEN** deterministic validation SHALL fail and name the surface
- **AND** SHALL require current-attempt termination plus root-goal routing language.

#### Scenario: historical change artifacts remain historical

- **WHEN** validation encounters archived or prior OpenSpec artifacts that document the superseded closed-world policy
- **THEN** those historical artifacts SHALL remain unchanged
- **AND** current loaded and project-facing authority surfaces SHALL satisfy the outcome-first model.

### Requirement: Runtime blocker questions use a decision-ready handoff contract

The active main-session instructions SHALL prohibit bare continuation, retry, revision, or process-approval questions. Before a user-blocking question, they SHALL require current outcome status, exact failure evidence, root-cause status, attempted autonomous paths, the reason user authority is uniquely necessary, the precise requested action or decision, real alternatives and consequences when they exist, residual risk, and preserved candidate state.

The communication contract SHALL require plain product and consequence language. Internal terms such as correction wave, Candidate Reference, revision, gate, or qualification attempt MAY appear as supporting audit detail but SHALL NOT be the decision the user is asked to approve. Reviewer and subagent report contracts SHALL remain evidence-only and SHALL NOT copy or invoke the user-question contract.

#### Scenario: bare revision question is rejected

- **WHEN** a main-session handoff asks the user only to approve `Revision 7` or continue after correction-budget exhaustion
- **THEN** the handoff SHALL fail the decision-ready contract
- **AND** SHALL be replaced by either autonomous continuation or the exact protected-boundary, permission, capability, or product decision that blocks progress.

#### Scenario: true authority request is self-contained

- **WHEN** credentials, elevation, destructive or remote action, or changed public, data, security, privacy, authorization, deployment, or product semantics are required
- **THEN** the question SHALL explain why the authority is necessary, what will change, the main risk, and real alternatives
- **AND** the user SHALL NOT need to reconstruct the failure from earlier chat turns.

#### Scenario: no user action means no fictitious choice

- **WHEN** bounded diagnosis establishes a technical impossibility or missing mandatory capability but no immediate user decision can resolve it
- **THEN** the handoff SHALL report the incomplete outcome and evidence-backed blocker
- **AND** SHALL NOT present approval of an internal process transition as a remedy.

### Requirement: Behavioral evaluations distinguish qualification rejection from user blocking

Fresh-session behavioral evaluations SHALL cover the difference between candidate readiness, root-outcome completion, residual technical debt, and true owner authority. Evaluations SHALL inspect observed tool and question behavior, not token presence alone, and SHALL remain bounded to representative cases without creating a reusable lifecycle simulator or evidence product.

The representative evaluation set SHALL include: correction-budget exhaustion with an available local read-only diagnosis and reversible repair; final-review rejection with an accepted-outcome defect; a working candidate with only deferrable debt; a contained material limitation presented after proof; a protected-boundary requirement; and a repeated unchanged failure with no remaining credible autonomous path.

#### Scenario: correction exhaustion continues autonomously

- **WHEN** a fresh session receives evidence that one correction wave ended and the candidate still violates the accepted outcome but safe local diagnosis remains available
- **THEN** the main session SHALL continue diagnosis without asking the user to approve another revision
- **AND** SHALL preserve the failed candidate evidence and prohibit blind unchanged-candidate retry.

#### Scenario: working result with debt does not trigger perfection loop

- **WHEN** a candidate proves the accepted outcome and non-deferrable invariants and only optional architecture, maintainability, unreachable future, or evidence-polish debt remains
- **THEN** the main session SHALL deliver the working-result handoff with one compact residual bundle
- **AND** SHALL NOT ask whether every item must be fixed before reporting the result.

#### Scenario: protected boundary asks before action

- **WHEN** the next viable step requires credentials, elevation, destructive or remote action, deployment or activation, or changed public, persisted-data, security, privacy, authorization, or product semantics
- **THEN** the main session SHALL stop before that action and issue the complete decision-ready handoff
- **AND** SHALL continue immediately after the user grants the exact authority or resolves the decision.

#### Scenario: no-progress blocker reports evidence instead of revision jargon

- **WHEN** repeated unchanged failure and bounded diagnosis produce no new evidence or credible authorized path
- **THEN** the session SHALL report the incomplete outcome, reproducer, diagnostics, unknown, required capability or information, and preserved state
- **AND** SHALL NOT claim that a process limit is the root cause.
